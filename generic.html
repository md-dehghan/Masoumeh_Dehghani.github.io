Hi, I’m

Physicist, Data Scientist

With wide experience in programming, algorithms, signal processing, mathematical metabolic modelling, computer simulations, data analysis and theoretical research. In order to develop my expertise in machine learning and deep learning, I recently enrolled into an intensive data science program with 550+ hours of courses. I’m excited to combine my background in Physics and my Data Science skills and contribute in performing robust analytics, building machine learning pipelines and making better decisions in industries and society. I enjoy deconvolving complex issues into tractable problems and leveraging data to offer solutions for challenges. My previous role as Post Doctoral Researcher led to the development of novel medical imaging techniques for studying brain function in humans and rodents. I also implemented signal processing and metabolic modelling to understand the interactions inside the brain using the MR signal from the brain. My top accomplishment: publishing 9 Peer-reviewed scientific articles and winning the two years young investigator grant competition held by the brain and behaviour research foundation. My research interests lie in the general area of machine learning, particularly deep representation learning to capture and understand the world.
